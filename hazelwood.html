Hazelwood Green Farmers Market
2022

This project was completed with Sydney Sun and Hannah Haytko-DeSalvo to design a farmers market for an upcoming urban redevelopment called Hazelwood Green.

This process involved a heavy focus on structural, enclosure, and MEP systems. The lightweight grid shell structure introduced a series of design challenges and solutions.

This project was completed under the supervision of Steve Lee at Carnegie Mellon University.
