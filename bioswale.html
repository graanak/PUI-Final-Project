Bioswale Housing Project
2021

This project was completed with Shray Tripathi to make a transition housing space in Aspinwall, Pittsburgh. The housing would serve for those doing environmental work in regards to watershed and pollution levels in the area.

This work engaged a series of mapping studies, form translations, and a proposal of integrating bioswale cultivation with a housing community.

This project was completed under the supervision of Dana Cupkova, Heather Bizon, and Jared Abraham at Carnegie Mellon University.
