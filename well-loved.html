Well Loved Shit: Clutter as Architecture
2022

This project focused analyzing the neighborhood Polish Hill in Pittsburgh, and constructing a series of narrative drawings.

After a series of mappings and neighborhood surveys, this project looks at clutter as space making on the porches of the residents, which balance between the realm of public and private.

This project was completed under the supervision of Tommy Yang at Carnegie Mellon University.
